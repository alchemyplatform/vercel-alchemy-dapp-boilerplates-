Introducing Elimuhub's Home-Based Tuition and Mentorship Services

At Elimuhub Education Consultants, we believe that every child deserves access to quality education and personal development, regardless of their circumstances. That's why we are excited to announce our new initiative: Home-Based Tuition and Mentorship Services for students residing in children's homes in Nairobi.

Why Home-Based Tuition and Mentorship?

The holiday season is a crucial time for students to recharge, reflect, and reinforce their learning. However, for many children in children's homes, this period can result in a lack of educational support due to limited resources and opportunities. Our home-based tuition and mentorship program is designed specifically to address these challenges by providing tailored educational support in a nurturing environment.
